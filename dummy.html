Understanding Market Dynamics:
# Trading involves buying and selling financial instruments such as stocks, currencies, commodities, and derivatives with the aim of generating profits. It operates on the principle of supply and demand, where prices fluctuate based on various factors like economic indicators, geopolitical events, and investor sentiment.
Types of Trading:
# Trading can be categorized into different types based on the duration of holding positions: Day Trading: Buying and selling financial instruments within the same trading day to capitalize on short-term price movements. Swing Trading: Holding positions for a few days to weeks, aiming to capture medium-term trends and price swings.
