Description: Trading refers to the buying and selling of financial instruments such as stocks, bonds, commodities, currencies, and derivatives with the aim of making a profit. It involves actively participating in the financial markets to capitalize on short-term price movements..


Description: Options trading is a form of investing or trading that involves contracts giving the holder the right, but not the obligation, to buy or sell an underlying asset at a specified price (the strike price) within a predetermined timeframe. These contracts are known as options, and they can be based on various underlying assets, including stocks, indices, commodities, and currencies.
